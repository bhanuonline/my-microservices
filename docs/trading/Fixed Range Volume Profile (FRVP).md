Fixed Range Volume Profile (FRVP) is a volume-based trading tool that shows where trading activity actually happened within a manually selected price range.
Instead of time, it focuses on price vs volume.

**Why it is important?**
Price moves because of volume, not indicators.
FRVP helps you identify:
📌 Strong support & resistance
📌 Fair value (balance area)
📌 Breakout vs fake breakout zones
📌 Institutional activity areas

🔹 2. Value Area (VA)
Usually 70% of total volume
VAH → Value Area High
VAL → Value Area Low

Meaning
Price inside VA → range / balance
Price outside VA → trend / imbalance
