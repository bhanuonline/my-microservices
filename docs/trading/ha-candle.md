📌 Setup (Intraday Option Buying)
Chart Type → Heikin-Ashi
Timeframe → 5 min or 15 min
EMA → 9 & 21
Use normal candle in another tab for exact price reference

🟢 CALL Option Setup (Buy CE)
Step 1: Trend Confirmation
Price above EMA 9 & EMA 21
EMA 9 > EMA 21
Consecutive green Heikin-Ashi candles
No lower wick in 2–3 candles

👉 Strong bullish momentum

Step 2: Entry
Enter when:
First strong green candle closes above recent high
Or small pullback candle forms then next green candle breaks it
Buy ATM or 1 step ITM option.

Step 3: Exit
Exit when:
First red Heikin-Ashi candle appears
OR candle with long upper wick appears
OR EMA 9 crosses below EMA 21

🔴 PUT Option Setup (Buy PE)
Step 1: Trend Confirmation

Price below EMA 9 & EMA 21
EMA 9 < EMA 21
Consecutive red candles
No upper wick

👉 Strong bearish momentum

Step 2: Entry
Enter when:
Strong red candle breaks recent low

Step 3: Exit
Exit when:
First green candle appears
Long lower wick forms
EMA crossover
