situation framing
"I identified that two developers with a technical dependency weren't communicating, which was creating a bottleneck for the team"

Actions you took
Conducted 1-on-1s with each developer to understand their perspective without bias
Reframed it as a technical problem — focused on the dependency, not the personal conflict
Facilitated a structured sync with a tight agenda around the interface/contract between their work
Defined clear ownership boundaries and documented responsibilities
Made the dependency visible on the board so the team could see the impact 

How you handled the ego aspect

Avoided taking sides or validating complaints about each other
Addressed behavior privately and professionally — set clear expectations around collaboration
